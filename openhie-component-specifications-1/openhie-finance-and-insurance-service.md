# OpenHIE Finance and Insurance Service (FIS)

FISF-08Finance and Insurance Service stores, categorizes, and facilitates the administration of centralised claims and finance related data to care provision to patients within the HIE. The service receives claims/financial data from Point of Service applications (including financing applications acting as a point of service interface outside of other PoS systems) and curates the management of them.

See also [Non-Functional Requirements](non-functional-requirements.md).&#x20;
